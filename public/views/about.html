WHO WE ARE

PelMakgabo Foundation is a non-profit charitable organisation proudly made for education empowerment for disadvantaged school children. We rely solely on donations, sponsors and community contributions. All financial and/or material resources are most welcome and heartily appreciated. The foundation mainly targets disadvantaged school children for educational, social, transformation and capacity building for impoverished community within the society system and class of South Africa. The foundation was founded in 2016 by Mapelo Makgabo (inspired by mother Johanna Makgabo as she identified disadvantaged learners in her school). In addition, it was created out of an unreserved burden and keen sense of responsibility by mostly disadvantaged school children from Townships in South Africa.

PMF is founded on the following pillars

Pillars

  • Founded to assist school children in need
  • Activities are conducted for the sake of the learners
  • Primary goal is the welfare and success of school children
  • The PMF strives to maintain the founding spirit and passion
  • We take the initiative and proactively pulling together resources to aid others

Mission

To enhance youth through social development programmes, continuous engagements and preparing as much youth as possible for the future ahead of them. To alleviate educational poverty in as many communities as we can.

Vision

To create the educational foundation that will not only touch and change lives of the disadvantaged children in various schools, but also to empower and prepare them for bigger and brighter futures.

Values

Honesty, integrity, consistency and the willingness to assist.
