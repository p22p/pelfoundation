PelMakgabo Foundation

PMF Background

Growing up as part of youth with different friends from different backgrounds, I have come to realize that failure isn’t caused by the fact that a person is unable to, but by the fact that they are emotionally drained and their backgrounds are affecting their productivity in front of other kids at school. As a lady who felt privileged enough to have my parents supporting me throughout my entire youth phase, I saw it a necessity to give back to the community, by being there for youth and upcoming generation is quiet a dream that I always had. Improving other people’s lives and motivating them to grow beyond circumstances. All nonprofits are not created equal. You're probably familiar with homeless shelters, religious organizations, animal rights groups, scientific organizations and other services that rely on volunteers and bleed money instead of making it. The terms nonprofit, charity and foundation are often used interchangeably and inaccurately. Nonprofit describes the entity's purpose: It's a business that's not operating to earn money for its owners or shareholders. PelMakgabo is looking at enriching the youth, through emotional support and moral support at the same time providing tools that will enable the youth to succeed at educational level.
